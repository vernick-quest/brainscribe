We're starting a little log of what we're building.

BrainScribe changes often — usually because a student, a parent, or a teacher told us something felt off, or could be better. This is where we'll write those changes down, in plain language, so you can see what's new and why.

## What you'll find here

- **New features** — when we add something to the coaches, the dashboards, or the writing flow.
- **Fixes** — when we squash something that wasn't working the way it should.
- **The thinking behind it** — a sentence or two on *why* a change was made, not just *what* changed.

## A couple of recent things

- **A guided first session.** New students now start with a short, no-pressure practice — talk through one short paragraph with Owen and end up with something you actually wrote. It's the fastest way to feel how the coaching works.
- **Coaches for everyone.** Parents and teachers can now write with a coach too, not just follow along — the same Socratic, your-words-only experience.

More soon. If there's something you'd like to see — or something that's bugging you — we'd genuinely like to hear it: **brainscribe.io@gmail.com**.
